Companion

A person who regularly travels a certain route and has expertise in navigating that journey. The companion offers guidance and support to ensure a smooth travel experience.

  • Provide detailed information about the trip.
  • Share tips and route details.
  • Assist with any travel-related issues.
  • Charge a fee for the provided services.

First-Time Traveler

Someone traveling that route for the first time who seeks guidance and support from the companion. They may need assistance with planning, navigating, and making the journey more comfortable and enjoyable.

  • Receive personalized travel assistance.
  • Get insights into the route and destination.
  • Have access to tips and recommendations from an experienced traveler.
  • Benefit from a smoother and more informed travel experience.

Carrier

A person who helps deliver packages for someone else. The carrier is responsible for ensuring that packages are transported safely and efficiently from sender to recipient.

  • Transport packages from one location to another.
  • Understand package details such as weight and size.
  • Ensure timely delivery based on agreed schedules.
  • Charge the sender for the delivery service.

Package Sender

Someone who needs to send a package and requires the carrier's assistance for delivery. The sender provides package details and coordinates with the carrier to ensure the package reaches its destination.

  • Provide details about the package, including weight and size.
  • Coordinate with the carrier for pick-up and delivery.

  • Track the package to ensure it reaches the recipient.
