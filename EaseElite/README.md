## 2ï¸âƒ£ Companion User

Companion users assist travellers during their journey.

### Actions:
- Create a profile and log in  
- Upload personal details and identity documents  
- Complete KYC verification  
- Select a subscription plan and make payment  
- Share travel itinerary and upload tickets  
- Receive and approve traveller requests  
- Meet travellers at the airport and accompany them to the destination  
- Submit feedback after completing the journey  

---

## 3ï¸âƒ£ First Time Traveller

First-time travellers receive guided and assisted travel support.

### Actions:
- Create a profile and log in  
- Select a plan and complete payment  
- Submit travel details and identity documents  
- Complete background verification and KYC  
- Get paired with an available companion  
- Receive companion details after approval  
- Meet the companion at the airport  
- Complete the journey with assistance  
- Submit feedback after travel  

---

## 4ï¸âƒ£ Carrier

Carriers are responsible for transporting packages securely.

### Actions:
- Create a profile and log in  
- Upload personal details, ID, and travel itinerary  
- Complete KYC verification  
- View incoming package requests  
- Review package details and proposed offers  
- Finalize pricing based on package weight and dimensions  
- Collect packages from the sender  
- Deliver packages to the recipient  

---

## 5ï¸âƒ£ Package Sender

Package senders use the platform to send packages through verified carriers.

### Actions:
- Create a profile and log in  
- Upload personal details and identity documents  
- Complete KYC verification  
- Submit package-related information  
- View and select suitable transport offers  
- Make payment based on package volume and weight  
- Hand over the package to the carrier  
- Track package status through the platform
